How It Works

Four steps. Zero prerequisites.

Think of an AI agent as a chef who can cook anything. A skill is the recipe. You're writing the recipe for work you already know how to do.

1

Record

Pick something annoying you do manually — a weekly report, a handoff note, a follow-up email.

2

AI writes the recipe

Describe the steps to an AI. It generates a structured skill file — your "recipe" — in seconds.

3

Test & refine

Run it with real data. Tweak the instructions until the output matches what you'd produce yourself.

4

Ship it

Your skill is ready. Use it daily. Share it with your team. Publish it for others.

2.5 hours. One working skill.

You'll leave with something that saves you 30+ minutes per week — starting tomorrow.
